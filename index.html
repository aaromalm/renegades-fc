Welcome to Renegades FC, a football club born in 2021, started by a group of friends from various regions of ernakulam with a shared passion for the game.

What began as weekend kickabouts soon grew into a brotherhood — united by teamwork, competitive spirit, and a love for football.

⚡ We play not for fame, but for the love of the game.

About Us

Renegades FC was founded in 2021 by a group of friends from Ernakulam who shared a simple but powerful bond — football.

With no professional background, no sponsors, and no official trophies, we started with weekend matches on local grounds and slowly formed our team.

As the years passed, our team grew — not just in numbers, but in unity and skill. We’ve played in many local tournaments, taken on tougher teams, and organized our own friendly matches and events in our community.

For us, football is more than a game — it’s a way to connect, grow, and stay grounded. Renegades FC is about friendship, hustle, and the joy of playing for the love of it.
